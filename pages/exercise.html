Why Pets Need Exercise

Exercise is essential for every pet, no matter their size or breed. Physical activity helps prevent obesity, strengthens muscles, and supports cardiovascular health. Beyond the physical benefits, exercise also provides mental stimulation, reducing stress and unwanted behaviors caused by boredom.

Dogs, for example, should be walked daily. Some breeds require longer runs, while others are content with short walks and playtime. Cats may enjoy chasing toys, climbing scratching posts, or exploring safe outdoor spaces. Even small pets, such as rabbits or guinea pigs, benefit from play areas that encourage movement and exploration.
