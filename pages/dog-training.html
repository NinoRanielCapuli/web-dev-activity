Basic Commands

Start with commands like sit, stay, and come. Use positive reinforcement like treats and praise.

House Training

Consistency is key. Take your puppy outside frequently and reward them for going in the right spot.

Socialization

Expose your pet to different people, places, and other animals to reduce anxiety and build confidence.
