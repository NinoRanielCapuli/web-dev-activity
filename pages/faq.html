Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I feed my dog?
Most adult dogs should be fed twice a day—once in the morning and once in the evening. Puppies need more frequent meals, typically 3–4 times a day.
How can I stop my cat from scratching furniture?
Provide scratching posts and pads around your home. Encourage use with catnip and rewards. Trim claws regularly to minimize damage.
When should I take my pet to the vet?
At least once a year for a check-up. Puppies, kittens, and senior pets may require more frequent visits.
Can dogs eat human food?
Some human foods are safe, like plain chicken or rice, but many—such as chocolate, grapes, and onions—are toxic. Always research before sharing food.
