Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I feed my dog?

Adult dogs usually need 2 meals a day, while puppies need 3–4 smaller meals.

Do indoor cats need exercise?

Yes, indoor cats need toys, scratching posts, and climbing areas to stay healthy.

What’s the best first pet for kids?

Calm breeds of cats or dogs, or small pets like guinea pigs, are usually recommended.
